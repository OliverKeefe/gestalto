# Reading List
## Sources

### Shortfalls of Centralized Computing Infrastructure
[1] ‘South Korea scrambles to restore digital services after server fire | Reuters’. Accessed: Oct. 09, 2025. [Online]. Available: https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/south-korea-restores-46-services-after-data-centre-fire-safety-minister-says-2025-09-29/

- News article detailing recent destruction of Republic of Korea's government datacenter due to faulty IPS battery.

### Networking and Cloud Computing
[2] A. Sheth, S. Padhee and A. Gyrard, "Knowledge Graphs and Knowledge Networks: The Story in Brief," in IEEE Internet Computing, vol. 23, no. 4, pp. 67-75, 1 July-Aug. 2019, doi: 10.1109/MIC.2019.2928449. keywords: {Photonic crystal fibers;Optical fiber losses;Propagation losses;Optical polarization;Network theory (graphs)},

- Paper detailing how Knowledge Graphs structure real-world information and, by evolving over time, play a vital role in modern AI applications such as recommendation, prediction, and question answering systems.

[3] Marcin Wylot, Manfred Hauswirth, Philippe Cudré-Mauroux, and Sherif Sakr. 2018. RDF Data Storage and Query Processing Schemes: A Survey. ACM Comput. Surv. 51, 4, Article 84 (July 2019), 36 pages. https://doi.org/10.1145/3177850

- Paper proposes a client-side symmetric key encryption method that secures cloud-stored files by encrypting data locally before upload and decrypting after download, improving both security and performance for large files.

[4] Peijin Cong, Guo Xu, Tongquan Wei, and Keqin Li. 2020. A Survey of Profit Optimization Techniques for Cloud Providers. ACM Comput. Surv. 53, 2, Article 26 (March 2021), 35 pages. https://doi.org/10.1145/3376917

- Survey that reviews techniques for optimizing cloud provider profits through improved service quality, pricing strategies, energy efficiency, and virtual network function deployment, offering insights into balancing cost reduction with performance in competitive cloud markets.

### Metadata
[5] J. Chen, O. Mashkova, F. Zhapa-Camacho, R. Hoehndorf, Y. He and I. Horrocks, "Ontology Embedding: A Survey of Methods, Applications and Resources," in IEEE Transactions on Knowledge and Data Engineering, vol. 37, no. 7, pp. 4193-4212, July 2025, doi: 10.1109/TKDE.2025.3559023. keywords: {Ontologies;Resource description framework;OWL;Semantics;Vectors;Machine learning;Surveys;Knowledge engineering;Logic;Cognition;Ontology;ontology embedding;web ontology language;knowledge graph;representation learning},

- Survey paper that reviews over 80 studies on ontology embedding, defining key concepts and categorizing methods to integrate machine learning with ontological reasoning, highlighting applications, tools like mOWL, and future research directions.

### Interplanetary File System and Web 3.0

[6] ‘IPFS Documentation | IPFS Docs’. Accessed: Oct. 09, 2025. [Online]. Available: https://docs.ipfs.tech/

- IPFS Official documentation outlining the functions of each of the protocols within the IPFS suite and how to apply them.

[7] ‘Why the Internet Needs the InterPlanetary File System - IEEE Spectrum’. Authors: Yiannis Psaras, Jorge M. Soares, David Dias. Accessed: Oct. 09, 2025. [Online]. Available: https://spectrum.ieee.org/peer-to-peer-network

- IEEE Spectrum magazine article explaining the advantages of IPFS as a distributed file storage system.

[8] J. Benet, ‘IPFS - Content Addressed, Versioned, P2P File System’, July 14, 2014, arXiv: arXiv:1407.3561. doi: 10.48550/arXiv.1407.3561.

- Paper detailing IPFS as a peer-to-peer distributed file system that uses content-addressed storage and a Merkle DAG structure to enable a decentralized, versioned, and fault-tolerant web without central points of failure.

[9] Y. Psaras, J. M. Soares and D. Dias, "To the InterPlanetary File System–and Beyond!: Peer-to-peer file sharing would make the Internet far more efficient," in IEEE Spectrum, vol. 59, no. 11, pp. 34-39, November 2022, doi: 10.1109/MSPEC.2022.9941036. keywords: {Interplanetary file system;Internet;IP networks;Browsers;Servers;Online services;Computer architecture;Logic gates},

- IEEE Spectrum magazine article explaining how P2P file sharing would improve the efficiency of the internet.

[10] ‘The GDP of IPFS: Measuring the Economic Impact of Decentralized Storage’, Pinata. Accessed: Oct. 10, 2025. [Online]. Available: https://pinata.cloud/blog/the-gdp-of-ipfs-measuring-the-economic-impact-of-decentralized-storage/

 - Industry report outlining the economic activity surrounding decentralized storage using IPFS, filecoin, Web3.0 and subsequent VC funding.

[11] Nizamuddin, N., Hasan, H.R., Salah, K. (2018). IPFS-Blockchain-Based Authenticity of Online Publications. In: Chen, S., Wang, H., Zhang, LJ. (eds) Blockchain – ICBC 2018. ICBC 2018. Lecture Notes in Computer Science(), vol 10974. Springer, Cham. https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-319-94478-4_14

- This paper presents a decentralized framework using IPFS and Ethereum smart contracts to ensure the authenticity and traceability of digital content, demonstrating a prototype for online book publishing that can extend to other multimedia domains.

### Encryption
[12] A. Musa and A. Mahmood, ‘Client-side Cryptography Based Security for Cloud Computing System’, in 2021 International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Smart Systems (ICAIS), Mar. 2021, pp. 594–600. doi: 10.1109/ICAIS50930.2021.9395890.

- This research introduces an improved client-side symmetric key encryption method for cloud storage that encrypts files locally before upload and decrypts them after download, enhancing security and performance for large data files.

[13] Martin Johns and Alexandra Dirksen. 2020. Towards Enabling Secure Web-Based Cloud Services using Client-Side Encryption. In Proceedings of the 2020 ACM SIGSAC Conference on Cloud Computing Security Workshop CCSW'20. Association for Computing Machinery, New York, NY, USA, 67–76. https://doi.org/10.1145/3411495.3421364

- This paper presents CryptoMembranes, a client-side framework that isolates encrypted user data from untrusted web application JavaScript, enabling secure end-to-end encryption for cloud-based web apps through native components or browser extensions.

### Information Security and Warfare

[14] M. C. Libicki, "Correlations Between Cyberspace Attacks and Kinetic Attacks," 2020 12th International Conference on Cyber Conflict (CyCon), Estonia, 2020, pp. 199-213, doi: 10.23919/CyCon49761.2020.9131731.

- This paper examines the relationship between cyber and physical conflicts, concluding that while cyber operations rarely escalate into kinetic warfare, real-world conflicts increasingly include cyber dimensions, suggesting an emerging threshold between non-lethal and lethal attacks.

[15] W. Duo, M. Zhou and A. Abusorrah, "A Survey of Cyber Attacks on Cyber Physical Systems: Recent Advances and Challenges," in IEEE/CAA Journal of Automatica Sinica, vol. 9, no. 5, pp. 784-800, May 2022, doi: 10.1109/JAS.2022.105548. keywords: {Industries;Process control;Control systems;Sensors;Internet;Security;Complex systems;Attack detection;attack strategy;cyber attack;cyber physical system (CPS);secure control}, 

- This paper surveys recent research on cyber-attacks targeting cyber-physical systems, analyzing availability, integrity, and confidentiality threats, along with corresponding defense strategies, and highlighting key challenges and future research directions.
